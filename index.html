The Many Federally Owned Lands of the American West

This map depicts US Federal Government owned lands of the eleven western United States. The topic has been mapped before, but this map is interactive (hover over lands for agency and tract name), shows proportional circles of the percent of Federal lands in each state, and it is presented with an appropriate map projection.

About the map

Use toggle below to switch the proportional circles on/off. Use the mouse to scroll zoom in/out and the map may be panned by holding down the left mouse button. Hover on the tracts or circles for additional information.

About the data

The National Map from the National Geological Survey is the source for the land polygon data. The Congressional Research Service's report Federal Land Ownership: Overview and Data was used for the information circle data. The 'Other Agencies' category is comprised of BOR, DOC, DOT, NASA, and USDA lands.
